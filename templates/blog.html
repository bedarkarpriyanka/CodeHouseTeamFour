A day in the Life @ VMWare - Christina Johnson

  1. Home
  2. Nina's Blog

Posted on January 1, 2017 at 12:00 PM

VMware is the global leader in virtualization solutions from the desktop to the datacenter. The company employs more than 22,000 people in 40 locations around the world, with VMware’s Irish office based in Ballincollig, Co. Cork, The company currently has a number of jobs available so we spoke to Sien Legouit who told us how the team at VMware work in an energetic environment based on creativity and collaboration.

What do you do?

As an Inside Sales Representative (ISR) I am primarily responsible for developing and driving business within the northern region of the Dutch market. From prospecting to closing deals leveraging the expertise of our Channel partners and the virtual team within VMware.

How did you get your job?

I started my career in IT at Techdata, a distributor as an Inside Sales Executive, selling Dell Enterprise products. I had the opportunity to develop my knowledge about datacentre solutions and doing business with channel partners which helps me a lot in my current role.

What’s the best thing about your job?

I get to speak to a lot of customers, some of them well-known companies in Holland, it’s great to hear and speak to them about solutions and share with them how VMware potentially could help them accelerate a business strategy. I am a saleswoman by heart, so closing complex deals with customers is something that really motivates me.
